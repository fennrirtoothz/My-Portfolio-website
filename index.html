Biography

Graphic design, video editing, and digital media design

Seksan Moolthaisong was born and raised in Khon Kaen, Thailand. He spent six years of his childhood in Phra Yuen District before moving into the city. He completed primary school at Tessaban Suansanuk School and secondary school in the Arts-Language program at Kaennakhon Wittayalai School.

During his studies, he began contributing translations and lyrics information through Musixmatch, with contributions published on Spotify and other connected music platforms. At the same time, he independently developed skills in graphic design, image editing, video production, music production, and web design.

He continues to expand his creative skills while preparing for future university study, focusing on polished visual work, digital experiences, and media with a clear identity.
